Microbiome Mondays

Microbes (little bugs) and their big data are my first love in scientific research since 2008. Trillions of these unicellular organisms thrive in one human adult. Earth in total harbors more microbes than the number of stars estimated in the universe. This micro-cosmos has been the source of drugs, diseases, food, polymers, supplements, fuel and countless other products that have shaped the advanced industrial age we currently live in. Their existence forces us to see every organism as an ecosystem. Through MicrobiomeMondays, an attempt was made to spread awareness about the story of these little bugs.
